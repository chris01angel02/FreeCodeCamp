Anchovy Fried Rice

To prepare a delicious and flavorful anchovy fried rice that is quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold rice)
  • 50 g dried anchovies
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 shallots, thinly sliced
  • 1 red chili, sliced (optional)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sweet soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • Sliced spring onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare all the ingredients.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a frying pan or wok over medium heat.
  3. Fry the dried anchovies until crispy, then remove and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, sauté the garlic, shallots, and chili until fragrant.
  5. Crack the egg into the pan and scramble it until fully cooked.
  6. Add the cooked rice and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Add the soy sauce, sweet soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), salt, and black pepper. Mix well until the rice is evenly coated.
  8. Return the crispy anchovies to the pan and stir until well combined.
  9. Cook for another 1-2 minutes while stirring continuously.
  10. Turn off the heat and garnish with sliced spring onions.
